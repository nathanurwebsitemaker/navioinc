Who We Are

Our Mission & Vision

We're on a mission to help businesses grow by matching the right talent to their specialized staffing and consulting needs.

Our Mission

Partnership built on trust, transparency & respect.

Our mission is to work in partnership and thoroughly understand the needs and preferences of both our clients and candidates — so we consistently deliver a recruitment service of an exceptional standard, guided by values of performance, trust, transparency, and respect.

We aim to create lasting relationships with our clients and candidates based on honesty, integrity, and mutual respect. We strive to be innovative and creative, adding value to the recruitment process at every opportunity.

We believe in diversity, so we work as a team. We share knowledge, experience, and leverage continuous improvement for learning in our IT consulting field.

Our Vision

Designed for success, engineered to achieve.

We want to be the leading partner that clients and candidates always choose to connect with — whether they need a single specialist or an entire team.

We will achieve our vision by partnering with clients to recruit, manage, and retain their workforce using our outstanding industry knowledge and deep talent networks.

Our Values

What we live and work for

Every person at Navio is guided and inspired by these shared values — from our first call with a client to the day a candidate starts their new job.

Integrity

We follow the highest standards of professional ethics, openness, and honesty. We don't promise what we cannot deliver.

Accountability

We take ownership and initiative in seizing opportunities, and we honor our promises and commitments to clients and candidates alike.

Teamwork

We are "One Team, One Dream" — drawing strength from our professional, cultural, and geographic diversity across the US.

Passion

We are passionate about our work, showing enthusiasm and energy in everything we do. We laugh a lot and love what we do!

Boldness

We are driven, ambitious, and enthusiastic. We're not afraid to learn from failure and we challenge the status quo to improve our service.

Community

We support our local Bay Area community and believe that helping businesses and workers connect creates a stronger region for everyone.

Our Approach

Recruitment that truly adds value

We know the job search can be challenging and overwhelming. That's why we're more than just recruiters — we're your career and hiring partners.

01

Understand your goals

We take time to understand what you truly want — not just the job title, but the culture, growth potential, and work environment that's right for you.

02

Match with precision

Our staffing model validates skill sets and experience, shortlisting only candidates who can genuinely add value. Solutions adapt to each client's culture.

03

Coach & prepare

We improve your resume, prep you for interviews, and give post-interview feedback — because your success is our success.

04

Place & support

We stay engaged after placement to ensure a smooth transition for both the candidate and employer. The relationship doesn't end at the offer letter.
