Career Tips

Make your resume stand out

Our recruiters have seen thousands of resumes. Here's what actually works:

  • Use resume keywords matched to the job description
  • Describe accomplishments, not just responsibilities
  • Quantify your achievements with numbers and results
  • Use a strong professional summary at the top
  • Tailor your resume to each job application
  • Add portfolio links or GitHub if relevant
  • Keep formatting clean — avoid walls of text
  • Use action verbs that communicate your impact
What to Avoid

Common mistakes to skip

These are the things that get resumes passed over — avoid them:

  • Too much information or irrelevant detail
  • Spelling mistakes or grammatical errors
  • Inaccuracies about your qualifications or experience
  • Unnecessary personal information (age, photo, etc.)
  • Negative comments about previous employers
  • Over-detailed hobbies and personal interests
  • Generic resumes sent to every job
  • Missing contact information or outdated email
