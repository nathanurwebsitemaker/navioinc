Resume Tips

Make your resume stand out

Our recruiters review every resume personally. Here's what helps you get noticed:

  • Use keywords from the job description — many systems scan for them
  • Lead with a strong objective or professional summary
  • Describe accomplishments, not just responsibilities — use numbers
  • Use action words: developed, led, built, improved, increased
  • Tailor your resume to each specific role you apply for
  • Keep it clean — no walls of text, spelling errors, or age/photo
  • Include certifications and relevant continuing education
FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Where is the Navio office located? +

Our office is located at 33878 Washington Ave, Union City, CA 94587. We serve clients and candidates across the entire United States.

Is there a cost to use Navio's recruiting service? +

No. Our service is completely free for job seekers. There is no charge for applying on our website or through LinkedIn. We earn only when we successfully place a candidate with an employer.

What types of contracts do you offer? +

We place candidates through W2 (direct employment) and C2C (Corp-to-Corp) contracts. We have both remote and on-site opportunities available.

Do you provide accounting or finance staffing? +

No. Our core expertise is in IT staffing — we provide IT consultants to Bay Area companies as well as clients across the USA and some international clients. We do not specialize in accounting or finance roles.

Why is the San Francisco Bay Area a great location to work in tech? +

Silicon Valley is a global center for high technology and innovation, located in the southern part of the San Francisco Bay Area. It's home to some of the world's most influential technology companies and offers unparalleled career opportunities for IT professionals.
