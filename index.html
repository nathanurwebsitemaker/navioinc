Who We Are

Your bridge between talent and opportunity.

Navio Inc is an IT staffing and recruiting company located in the Union City Bay Area, CA. We act as the bridge between clients, employers, and employees — identifying top talent and presenting only the best candidates.

The job market has changed considerably over recent years and the hiring process is longer and more difficult than ever. Working with a staffing agency like Navio makes it quicker, smarter, and easier.
